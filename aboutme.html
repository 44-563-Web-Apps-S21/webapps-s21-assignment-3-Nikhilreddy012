Heatblast

The mission of Heatblast is to kill Vilgax who is trying to steal the omnitrix from its creator. Heatblast is a plasma-based life-form whose body is composed of a super hot inner plasma body covered by a dark red or brown rocks. Heatblast has the ability to generate and manipulate intense heat and fire from his body. He can form fire into any shape that he chooses, commonly fireballs. He can enhance the fire in his hands for flaming punches.
