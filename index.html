Playing Cricket

There are 11 players in a team of cricket. The bowler bowls the ball and the batsmen has to hit the ball with the bat. You can run between the wickets after hitting or get 4 or 6 runs if the ball reaches the boundary. The team which scores more number of runs wins the game. Cricket is undoubtedly the biggest sport in India and I always love playing it. Sachin Tendulkar who is also known as the God of cricket is my all time favourite player. He has made me like the sport even more.
